History of LUMINAL

LUMINAL was founded in 2024 by four talented individuals who shared a passion for music and performance. Over the years, they have grown from local stars to global icons, captivating audiences worldwide with their electrifying presence and unique style.

The group debuted with their hit single "ENERGY", which quickly climbed the charts and established them as a force to be reckoned with. Their journey has been marked by innovation, creativity, and dedication to their craft, earning numerous awards and accolades along the way.

From sold-out concerts to chart-topping albums, LUMINAL continues to inspire fans and set new standards in the music industry.
